Professional Journey

Camlist (2023 - 2025)

As a Senior Software Engineer at Camlist, Scott Fernald led the development of a React Native mobile app for pet buyers and sellers. He integrated AI-powered chat, real-time video calls, and pet financing options, revolutionizing how people find and adopt pets online.

  • Built and launched Camlist’s marketplace app on iOS & Android.
  • Developed AI-driven chatbots for buyer-seller interactions.
  • Integrated live video calls, enabling remote pet showcasing.
  • Optimized cloud performance using AWS, Kubernetes, and microservices.
Upwork (2022 - 2023)

As a top-rated freelancer on Upwork, Scott specialized in AI, LLMs, and automation solutions. He worked with businesses to integrate OpenAI-powered chatbots, NLP models, and AI-driven automation into their operations.

  • Developed AI-driven chatbots and automation tools.
  • Built scalable AI-powered data processing systems.
  • Integrated OpenAI APIs and deployed cloud-based AI solutions.
Rover (2017 - 2022)

At Rover, Scott played a key role in building pet-sitter and pet-owner platforms. As a Senior Software Engineer, he focused on React, Python, and cloud-based microservices to enhance Rover’s customer experience.

  • Developed and optimized Rover’s pet-owner and sitter experience.
  • Worked with React, Python, PostgreSQL, and AWS.
  • Championed automated testing and CI/CD best practices.
Zoetis (2011 - 2017)

Scott started his career at Zoetis, leading software development for bio-devices and automation systems in the veterinary industry. He integrated machine learning, computer vision, and cloud computing into advanced pet healthcare solutions.

  • Built a microservices-based SaaS platform for pet clinics.
  • Worked with Java, Spring, Kubernetes, Azure, and MongoDB.
