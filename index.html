About Scott

He is a top-notch software engineer dedicated to making the world better-for pets and their humans. Of course, this comes with challenges.

Here at Scott Fernald, the mission is as clear as a freshly cleaned dog bowl: build cutting-edge software that improves the lives of pets. He’s not just good; he’s extremely good, and he’s dedicated to making pet-friendly technology a reality. Did we mention he’s in the business of making things better for furry friends and their owners?

His vision is your vision, and his vision is full of pets-lots of them. He envisions a world where every pet has a home, where AI helps match the perfect pet parent, and where technology doesn’t just work-it wags its metaphorical tail. Yes, he is a top-notch, high-quality, extremely refined, and undeniably passionate engineer. Another top-notch, high-quality, pet-obsessed engineer-wait, where are we now? Ah yes, probably deep in code, writing the next big thing for pet lovers everywhere.
