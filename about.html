About Me

A software engineer who loves pets, builds cool things, and believes technology should have heart.

If there’s one thing Scott Fernald knows, it’s that life is better with pets-and great technology. He’s the kind of guy who will spend hours debugging a piece of code just to make sure an app runs perfectly, all while his own pets stare at him, silently judging (or maybe just waiting for snacks).

With over a decade of experience in full-stack development, AI, and cloud solutions, he has worked on everything from AI-powered pet adoption platforms to seamless mobile applications that make life easier for pet lovers everywhere. His secret ingredient? A mix of problem-solving, innovation, and probably a little pet hair on his keyboard.

When he’s not building technology that actually makes a difference, he’s probably outside chasing after his own pets, brainstorming ways to make the world better for animals-and their humans.

About Me

Turning ideas into reality-one line of code (and one pet adoption) at a time.

Scott has built platforms used by thousands of pet lovers worldwide, working with companies like Camlist, Rover, and Zoetis to create seamless, intuitive digital experiences. Whether it’s developing an AI-driven chat system for pet adoption or optimizing cloud services to handle millions of users, he makes sure every project runs as smoothly as a happy pup on a morning walk.

But his work isn’t just about writing code-it’s about solving real-world problems. He believes the best technology should feel effortless, making life easier and more enjoyable. No unnecessary complexity, no frustrating user experiences-just smart, powerful solutions that help people (and pets!) live better.

And the best part? He’s just getting started. Whether it's launching new AI features, optimizing cloud systems, or finding new ways to connect people with their future pets, there’s always a new challenge waiting. And he wouldn’t have it any other way.
